## Our Music Library

Here you can find the sheet music and scores we use for our Masses and special celebrations. Choir members can download and print these for rehearsal.

---

### 📋 How to Use This Page

1. Click on a score title to download the PDF
2. Print it or view it on your device
3. Bring it to rehearsal!

*To add a new score: place the PDF in `assets/scores/` and add a link below.*

### How to Add New Scores

1. Save your PDF score into the folder: `assets/scores/`
2. Open the file `music-scores.md`
3. Add a new row to the right table following the same format
4. Save and upload — it will appear here automatically!
