## Join the Chorale Saint Padre Pio

Do you love music? Do you want to serve your parish? **We'd love to have you!**

The Chorale Saint Padre Pio welcomes new members throughout the year. You don't need to be a professional singer — all you need is a willing heart and a love for God.

---

## Who Can Join?

- **Any parishioner** of Paroisse de Kansanga
- People of **all ages** (youth, adults, and seniors)
- **Beginners and experienced singers** alike
- Anyone who wants to **grow in faith and music**

---

## What to Expect

✅ **Weekly rehearsals** — We meet every **Saturday** from **[time TBA]** at the parish hall  
✅ **Sunday Mass** — We sing at the main Sunday liturgy  
✅ **Special celebrations** — Feast days, weddings, funerals, and diocesan events  
✅ **A welcoming community** — We are a family united in prayer and music

---

## What We Ask of Members

- Attend rehearsals regularly and come prepared
- Arrive **15 minutes early** before Mass
- Dress modestly and in choir uniform for performances
- Participate with a spirit of prayer and reverence
