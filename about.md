## Who We Are

The **Chorale Saint Padre Pio** is the official choir of Paroisse de Kansanga, Uganda. We are a community of faithful singers united by our love for God and our dedication to enriching the liturgical life of our parish through sacred music.

Named after **Saint Padre Pio of Pietrelcina** — a man of profound prayer, humility, and love for the Church — we are inspired by his example to offer our voices as a gift to God and our community.

---

## Our Mission

Our mission is simple: **to praise God through music, and to help the congregation enter more deeply into worship.**

We believe that music is not merely decoration for the Mass — it is an integral part of the liturgy itself. When we sing, we pray twice.

---

## Our Repertoire

We sing a rich variety of sacred music, including:

- **Traditional Latin hymns** — Kyrie, Gloria, Sanctus, Agnus Dei
- **Contemporary African Catholic music** — songs in Luganda and Swahili
- **Choral arrangements** for feast days and special occasions
- **Responsorial Psalms** for Sunday Masses

---

## Our History

Founded in **2010**, our choir began with a small group of dedicated parishioners. Over the years we have grown in number, skill, and spirit. We have had the privilege of performing at diocesan events and representing our parish in wider church celebrations.

Today, we welcome singers of **all skill levels and backgrounds** who feel called to serve through music.

---

## Leadership

Our choir is led by a dedicated team including our **Musical Director**, **Accompanist**, and **Choir President**, who work together to ensure every liturgy is prepared with care and reverence.

> *"Singing is praying twice."* — Saint Augustine
